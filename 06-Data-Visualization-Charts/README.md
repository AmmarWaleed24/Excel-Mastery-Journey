# 📈 Lesson 06: Data Visualization & Charts

## 🎯 Objectives
Turning complex numbers into visual stories that are easy to understand at a glance.

---

## 🛠 Skills Applied
- **Column & Bar Charts:** Best for comparing different categories (e.g., Sales by Product).
- **Line Charts:** Perfect for showing trends over time (e.g., Monthly Revenue).
- **Pie Charts:** Used to show the composition of a whole (e.g., Market Share).
- **Combo Charts:** Combining two different data types (e.g., Total Sales as Bars vs Profit Margin as a Line).
- **Chart Design:** Customizing colors, labels, and axes for a professional look.

## 📊 Practical Application
In the file `06-Charts-Practice.xlsx`, I created a visual summary of the sales data:
1. A **Trend Line** showing sales growth over 12 months.
2. A **Comparison Bar Chart** for top-performing employees.
